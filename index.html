About Us

North Wales Drum School offers drum tuition for all ages - from beginners to seasoned players!

Students have a chance to get behind the kit from the very first lesson to start learning the introductory skills needed to become an accomplished player, all from our purpose built home studio in Buckley, North Wales. We offer private lessons in which the student will learn the core skills such as:

  • Technique
  • Reading
  • Rudiments
  • Co-ordination
  • Knowledge of Rhythms & Styles
Rockschool & Trinity grades up to 8 available.
Please contact me for rates and availability.
